2017 Contemporaries Bash:
Berlin Underground

The Phillips Collection invites you to the 2017 Contemporaries Bash: Berlin Underground. Inspired by Berlin’s pulsing nightlife, the Bash brings the German capital’s decadent club scene to Dock 5 in one sensational night of cocktails, music, food, fashion, and dancing.

The Bash is a sell-out event that attracts the city’s most dynamic young professionals. Come connect with The Phillips Collection’s community of art-lovers, creative thinkers, and philanthropists.

Featuring:
Premium open bar and outdoor bier garten with Deni
Savory and sweet bites inspired by Berlin’s street food
DJ, Photo booths, art-installations, and more

For more information, contact us

Must be 21 or older · Black tie or cocktail attire · Free parking

2016 Contemporaries Bash: Dreaming of Doha

Guests at the 2016 Contemporaries Bash: Dreaming of Doha were treated to an immersive cultural experience highlighting Doha’s vibrant colors and the rich culture of Qatar and the Middle East. The dazzling night featured falconry, gaming stations, an Arabic calligrapher, henna stations, and an unforgettable art installation by Daniel Wurtzel. Join the Contemporaries

Fostering Learning Through Art

The Annual Gala and Contemporaries Bash raise critical resources for the museum’s award-winning education programs that inspire creativity, foster a greater understanding of our world, and provide lifelong learning experiences for art-lovers of all ages in the Washington, DC, community and across the country.
